About Us

With a friendly nod to the struggles of the first-year experience, CCS13 was born out of the shared journey of BSCS and BSIT freshmen at the University of Perpetual Help System. The creators, armed with a genuine desire to simplify the complexities of CCS1101, bring you a concise rendition of your course material.

No grandiose missions or over-the-top ambitions—just a bunch of students who've been there, done that, and want to help you do it too. Think of us as your virtual study buddy, here to make your Introduction to Computing coursework a tad less perplexing.

So, gear up for a journey through the realms of computing, coding, and all things CCS1101. Let's tackle CCS13 together, one byte at a time!
